Frequently Asked Questions
How often does Stratavor sync my accounting data?
By default, Xero and QuickBooks connections sync every 4 hours automatically. You can trigger a manual sync at any time from the Integrations page. HubSpot and other CRM connectors sync every 6 hours. If you need more frequent syncing, contact support to discuss real-time options.
How do I generate a new Financial Snapshot?
Navigate to Reports, select the Financial Snapshot tab, and click "Generate Financial Snapshot." Stratavor will pull the latest data from your connected sources, run variance analysis against your uploaded budget, and produce a board-ready presentation. Generation typically takes 2–3 minutes.
What file types can I upload to the Document Vault?
The Document Vault accepts .xlsx, .csv, .pdf, .docx, .pptx, and .json files up to 50MB each. Spreadsheet files are parsed for structured data (budgets, forecasts, risk registers), while PDFs and documents are indexed for AI-powered narrative enrichment and contextual analysis.
Can I connect multiple accounting platforms simultaneously?
Yes. You can connect Xero, QuickBooks, and Sage concurrently. Stratavor will map each source independently and you can choose which source feeds each report type. This is useful during accounting platform migrations or for multi-entity consolidation.
How is my data secured?
All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We use row-level security in our database layer so each organisation's data is fully isolated. OAuth tokens are stored in encrypted vaults and never exposed. Stratavor is SOC 2 Type II compliant and undergoes annual penetration testing. See our Security & Compliance page for full details.
What does the Intelligence Hub AI have access to?
The AI assistant can query your synced accounting data, uploaded documents, generated reports, and strategic profile. It cannot access data from other organisations, make changes to your accounts, or execute financial transactions. All AI conversations are logged and available in your account history.
