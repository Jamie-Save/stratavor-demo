Gross Margin Decline — October 2025

Your gross margin fell to 34.2% in October, down from 38.7% in September. That's a 4.5 percentage point decline, which translates to approximately €42,800 in lost margin on broadly similar revenue levels.

The primary driver was a €67,300 increase in cost of goods sold, concentrated in two areas: a supplier price adjustment on your core SaaS hosting infrastructure (effective 1 October) and a one-off professional services cost related to your Q3 client implementation sprint.

Given your stated priority of operational efficiency, this trend warrants attention. While the one-off implementation cost won't recur, the hosting price adjustment is structural and will compress margins going forward unless renegotiated or absorbed through pricing.

Recommended Next Step
Consider reviewing your supplier contracts this quarter — a 5% reduction in COGS would recover approximately €18,000 in margin. Alternatively, evaluate whether your current pricing absorbs the increased cost base.
